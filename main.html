Summary

Inception is a 2010 science fiction film written and directed by Christopher Nolan. The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, a professional thief who steals information by infiltrating the subconscious of his targets. He is offered a chance to have his criminal history erased as payment for the implantation of another person's idea into a target's subconscious.

Actors

The movie features an ensemble cast including:

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal Cobb

Favorite Quote
