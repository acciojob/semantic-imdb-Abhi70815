Summary

Race 3 is a high-octane action thriller that follows the lives of the Singh family, a ruthless clan involved in the world of crime. Betrayal, deceit, and power struggles are at the core of this gripping tale as the family members navigate their way through intense rivalries and unexpected alliances. The movie features spectacular stunts, exotic locations, and a fast-paced storyline that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats.

Actors

The film stars Salman Khan as Sikander Singh, the charismatic and powerful leader of the Singh family. The ensemble cast includes Anil Kapoor, Bobby Deol, Jacqueline Fernandez, Daisy Shah, and Saqib Saleem, each bringing their unique flair and energy to their respective roles.
